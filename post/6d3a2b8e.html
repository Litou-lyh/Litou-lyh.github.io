The Trip in California

Date: Dec 24, 2023 -> Jan 3, 2024

Friends:

- Chengxi Guo @ UCSD
- Chenang Li @ UCSD
- Jialu Liang @ UCSD
- Yutao Qiu @ USC

Departure

Dec 24, 19:00

Alaska Airline - Boeing 737 Max 9 - 5 h 40 m

Arrive San Diego at 21:40

A really fast flight, but comfortable

Day 1

Chengxi and I visited UCSD. It is a beautiful campus!

The Bear of CSE @ UCSD, interesting and cute!

The Falling Star!

The cool guy Chengxi standing on the head of rattlesnake, near the library of UCSD.

We also visited a beach near their college:

Day 2

Chengxi and I visited The Midway Museum @ sd. It was an aircraft carrier named after the famous Midway battle. And also the statue of “The victory kiss”. A fun fact is that, the Victory Kiss happened on Times Square, New York. So basically, I came from the place this event took place, to see the statue in memory of that event.

Day 3

Chenang and Jialu joined us, and we called a lyft ride to LA.

We first visited University of South California. Yutao is our old friend and also the local guide! It’s a old-fashioned campus, not so surprising, but still a beautiful one. At least better than NYU, because we have no “campus”.

The USCers call them Trojans. We heard the story of the football games between Trojans and the team from UCLA. Hope they will win a game one day!

We than went to a nearby museum, where I saw the most realistic animal exhibition in my life.

The trick is to set a circular background, when you are standing at the center of that circle, the distance to every angles of the background are the same!

Day 4

We went to Universal Studio, which is actually a amusement park with roller coasters. I took 2 roller coasters and two 4D rides. Those are horrible. Only when I was on those rides, do I understand why people will stream out loud. Because you cannot help to scream! You have to close your eyes and scream to release the severe tense of high g-values. But I love the IP and the scenes in the park. What a day!

Day 5

We visited Warner Bros Studio and took pictures of the well-known scenes of Friends and The Big Bang Theory. It way worthy to visit, but not surprising to me.

Around sunset time, we managed to arrive at Griffith Observatory. Although We did not witness the sunset because of clouds, we saw the Hollywood sign on the distant mountain, streams of car lights in the evening streets, and sunset pilgrimage by devout Muslims.

We took some interesting pictures!

Another interesting thing is: I have thought the Mt. Wilson Observatory was way more important than the Griffith, but I changed my mind. I weighed the Mt. Wilson for its contribution in the development of Cosmology, and I thought the Griffith was famous only because of the scene in the movie La La Land. Now I am aware that Mr. Griffith was inspired by the Mt. Wilson, and then the Griffith further inspired millions of children as well as scientists. Salute!

Day 6

We hired a driver to send us to Yosemite. The foggy and cloudy Yosemite is the best!

Day 7

We went hiking, tired but happy.

Day 8

We headed to San Francisco. We took pictures on Golden Gate Bridge and sunset at twin peaks.

Day 9

It’s a rainy day. We walked around the Golden Gate Park (wish Central Park can be beautiful as this one). Chengxi and I watched NBA game (GSW vs ORL). SC30 MVP !!!

Return

United Airline - Boeing 777-200ER

13:15 (-8) - 21:50 (-5)

Tired. Finally home.

This is a wonderful trip once a life!
