About Me

I am currently a second year Master’s student of Computer Science at New York University. I graduated from the Chinese University of Hong Kong, Shenzhen in 2022. I am open to all opportunities, Software Development, Data analytics, Machine Learning, and AI research, either in China or USA.

I coded with C/C++, Java, Python, and so on. View my projects in my resume.

I did research in Computer Vision at GAP Lab in CUHKSZ, supervised by Prof. Xiaoguang Han. Later, I interned in Xiaobing.ai as a computer vision research intern.

My ideal work place is NYC or The Bay Area.

Feel free to contact me!

我目前是纽约大学计算机科学专业二年级硕士生。我于 2022 年毕业于香港中文大学（深圳）。我对所有机会持开放态度，无论是在中国还是在美国，包括软件开发、数据分析、机器学习和人工智能研究。

我使用 C/C++、Java、Python 等进行编码。在我的简历中查看我的项目。

我在香港中文大学（深圳）GAP Lab从事计算机视觉方面的研究，导师是韩晓光教授。后来我在小冰实习，担任计算机视觉研究实习生。

首选工作地点为上海或杭州。我是嘉兴人。
